The poetess Natalya Nagornyak

Posted on Sunday January 24th, 2016 by Anatoliy Zolotukhin

January 24, 2016 in Nikolaev Pushkin club hosted a recital of the Secretary of the club and the poetess Natalya Nagornyak. At the party in the author’s performance sounded poems written at different times. Musical accompaniment of the evening provided a musical ensemble: the head of Valery Zelinskiy (electric guitar), Paul Nagornyak (acoustic guitar) and Sergey Fin’ko (drums).
