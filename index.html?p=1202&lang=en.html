10 Precepts of the philosophy of dynamic dualism

Posted on Sunday July 5th, 2020 by Anatoliy Zolotukhin

Report at the II International Scientific Conference on June 26-27, 2020

  1. It is absurd to think that we are smarter than God the Father, as the personified World Mind, uniformly isotropically scattered in intergalactic space, maximally ordered by Him, at the lowest absolute temperature (Relict radiation!). But it is even more absurd to think that there is no God. The 10 commandments given to Moses are the Laws of God, which act in a society like a boomerang: “Therefore, all things whatsoever ye would that men should do to you, do ye even so to them: for this is the law and the prophets”. (Matt. 7:12). Faith in God is a purely personal and self-sufficient matter: “For the kingdom of God is within you.” (Luke 17:21) God has no governors or mediators on earth. Christ, as a man, was crucified for intercession and Rose as the Son of God. Among the 12 teachers of the faith: the 10th will be Peter, the 11th will be Judas, the 12th will be Thomas, and the 13th will be Paul.
  1. God the Father is Truth, Conscience and Love. God is not a dictator. He hesitates, doubts, and therefore is always paradoxical and fair. He divided Himself into two equal parts: the Spiritual and the Material, neither of which can be either primary or defining (The first philosophical question that is primary: Matter or Spirit? – foolish question!). The main condition for their existence everywhere and in everything is the constant transition from one part to another. Eternal movement (the Holy Spirit) is the third incarnation of God, which scientists have called the Time or Frequency of oscillations. Therefore, everything in Nature develops cyclically: “To everything, there is a season, and a time to every purpose under the heaven: A time to be born, and a time to die…” (Ecclesiastes 3: 1-2). The Conception and Resurrection of Christ, the Star of Bethlehem (UFO), the Walking on the Waters, the Transfiguration, the Ascension of the Cross, sleep and hypnosis are evidence of the possibility of a rapid transition of a living organism from the spiritual (virtual) to the material (real) world and vice versa. Life is the temporary maintenance of the balance of oscillations between these two worlds of a living being. Therefore, the principle of additionality discovered by N. Bohr must be fulfilled in any substance: Matter X Spirit = const! It fixes the internal conditions of the transition of parts of the Matter-Spirit complex from one to another and is a new formulation of the first philosophical question.
  1. The second philosophical question concerns: is it possible to know the nature of the double complex Matter-Spirit as such? Heisenberg’s uncertainty principle already applies here: ∆ Matter X ∆ Spirit = const! It fixes the fundamental impossibility of defining the Matter-Spirit complex because the deeper we know ∆ Spirit, the more we will destroy Matter in this complex and vice versa. That is, we are talking about fundamental agnosticism, in which you can delve as much as you want in the study of each part of the complex separately, but the error in the acquired knowledge about the Matter-Spirit complex will grow. This means, first, that Truth in any science has a statistical and probabilistic form. Secondly, behind this is God, without whose faith it is impossible to delve into the knowledge of the Matter-Spirit complex: “Now faith is the substance of things hoped for, the evidence of things not seen.” (Heb. 11: 1) Faith is an essential element of sustainable development, which allows overcoming the fundamental particulars agnostics. The Science is borrowed refutation divinities, and the God is concentrated mostly in Ignorance, ie in a dark matter and dark energy. “For the things which are seen are temporal, but the things which are not seen are eternal“. (2 Cor. 4:18) The desire to know the Truth – is Love. And, as Goethe said: “The pursuit to Truth – more beautiful of owning Her. “
  1. God is fully contained in us and in our subconscious: “…because greater is He Who is in you, then he who in the world” (1 John 4: 4). He is invisible and silent. The existence of God can be judged by the knowledge that has been revealed to us: “Fear them not therefore: for there is nothing covered, that shall not be revealed; and hid, that shall not be known“. (Matthew 10:26) God never directly affirms or acts directly. This is the busy science. God communicates with us through Music, Poetry, paradoxical foreign language, which raise doubts, which, together with practice and faith, are the engines of science. Today there are voices (I. Musk) that the artificial super mind ASI (Artificial Super Intelligence) can defeat a living person. The ultimate goal of humanity, as an organic high-temperature civilization OHTC (Organic High-Temperature Civilization), maybe the transformation of the ASI set into a low-temperature inorganic civilization LTIC (Low-Temperature Inorganic Civilization). Such an LTIC will exist almost forever due to the presence of superconductivity and superfluidity on distant cold planets. Due to the use of nanotechnology, the scale of ASI can be too small and the amount of information accumulated over millions of years of existence can be too large. Externally, such LTIC can dissolve in Nature.
  1. The second of the 10 Commandments of God should be supplemented with the words: “Do not make of yourself, and then – for yourself an idol.” Be honest with yourself first, and therefore fair. Conscience is a fraction, in the numerator: the sum of the demands on yourself, and in the denominator – on others, multiplied by the number of people around you. Therefore, any government is a priori shameless. Remember that it is impossible to live without faith in God, for “For we walk by faith, not by sight” (2 Cor. 5: 7). We remember that the highest reward for each of us should be only the Name given to us by God, not by authority, that “wisdom is a mind insisted on conscience” (F. Iskander).
