RAMIRO ARISTA + ARS NOVA

Posted on Saturday June 9th, 2018 by Anatoliy Zolotukhin

On June 9, 2018, a concert of ARS NOVA chamber orchestra under the leadership of conductor Ramiro Arista (Argentine) took place in the Palace of Culture of Builders within the framework of the 81st season of the Mykolayiv Regional Philharmonic. The concert featured works: V.A. Mozart, A. Romer, E. Vila-Lobos, K. Santoro, C. Guerra-Peuge, A. Piazzolla. A concert was performed by Honored Art Worker of Ukraine, art critic Tamara Piven’.                                                                                                      This is the second concert of Ramiro Arista in Mykolaiv. This time, Maestro has comforted the Mykolayiv Mozart-Bahian program, which was performed masterfully in pastel-velvet tones by our perfect chamber orchestra “ARS NOVA”, the game of which was strengthened at the end of our concert by our famous ensemble of folk instruments “Uzory(Patterns)”. The remarkable artistic and expressive manner of conducting Ramiro Aristo should be discussed separately. Because nothing can easily destroy the barriers between musicians and the public, like the liberty, frankness and sincerity of the conductor. Amazingly warm and sincere concert!
