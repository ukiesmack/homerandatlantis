The second “Olbia Forum” (in memory of V.V. Krapivina) is the 2nd day

Posted on Saturday May 5th, 2018 by Anatoliy Zolotukhin

On May 5, 2018, the Second International Scientific and Practical Conference “Olvia Forum”, devoted to the memory of Valentina Krapivina, continued in the Nikolayev Regional Museum of Local Lore. The organizers of the event were the Ministry of Education and Science of Ukraine, the Institute of Archeology of the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine, the National Academy of Sciences of Ukraine. V.A. Sukhomlinsky, Nikolayev Regional Museum of Local History and Sciences and Research Archaeological Reserve “Olvia”.
