APOCRYPHOLOGY AS A TOOL OF CONTINUOUS EDUCATION

Posted on Sunday February 7th, 2021 by Anatoliy Zolotukhin

In “The Scythian Odyssey”, Lina Kostenko posed key and paradoxical questions for understanding the history of Ukraine in the context of the paradigm adopted today about Greek colonization: “Science is deep, and work is titanic. Mystery, unsolved long ago – was it an ethnic expansion, or is it a union of related tribes?”, “What was the law here? Why didn’t the Scythians create the alphabet? For only the Word is the memory of salvation. So only living people, who had their own letter! Could it be that they had such writing that we would not read it? And who are they? And who are we? Who are we? Who are we? Who are our ancestors? Did you come? Natives? ”

The new science of apocryphology [1] provides accurate answers to these prophetic questions. First, Ukrainians are not immigrant people, but natives from the times of Trypillia and Sabatyn culture, and then Cimmerians-Atlanteans. Secondly, Atlantis was located in the Crimea near the modern city of Evpatoria, it according to Homer lasted more than a thousand years – it was the first ship power in Europe, which united the ports of the Black, Marble, Aegean and the eastern Mediterranean and died on October 8. 1447 BC [3a]. During the existence of Atlantis, an alliance of related tribes of Cimmerian, Greek, and Phoenician sailors were formed. Third, Homer writes that 10 families of Atlanteans on 10 ships survived 5 days before the disaster. [3b] They founded a city in Mykolayiv, which Homer called Alibant in “The Odyssey”, which he explored (the Wild Garden archaeological site). From here they sailed to the Greeks for half a millennium.

It is unknown why in the IX century B.C. the Cimmerians marched across the Caucasus to Asia, and their place was taken by the Scythians, who came down from the Caucasus, not from the Altai steppes. The city of Alibant was dead at this time, so it was called Hades. When the Cimmerian soldiers returned home 30 years later, they were not accepted by their wives and children, who already lived with the Scythians. The Cimmerians were knights, did not fight with relatives, but let go half-shaven head (oseledets) as a sign of exile, and returned to Asia Minor. In 777 BC. Cimmerian king and sailor Gastiy, married to the Egyptian queen Philainia, remembering his Atlantic origin, decided to return from Cappadocia (Turkey) to his native Alibant (Mykolayiv) with his pregnant wife, daughter and two sons. When they arrived in Alibant, on the first day of the new year, July 16, 676 B.C., Targitay was born here, according to the “History” of Herodotus, as if the founder of the Scythian family. In fact, the Cimmerian Targitay of Gileya was the founder of the Scythian state. Since the basis of this state were nomads Scythians, Sarmatians and other Scythian tribes, in 592 B.C. Cleopatra renamed Gileya to Scythia, her and Homer’s son of Gil to Scyth, and her Cimmerian family Targitay to Royal Scythians. But the renaming does not change the genetic code and therefore should be considered the founders of Antiquity and Christianity of the Cimmerians, not the Scythians.

The sailor Targitay in 15 created the Greek alphabet together with the Phoenician king Cadmus on the basis of the Phoenician. But the version developed by Cadmus did not take root (probably with little practice of use), but the version of Targitay was fixed. He traded grain with Greece, as in the days of Atlantis, and as early as 18 was part of Pritaniya in Athens, as head of one of the 10 phyla called Gileya. And in 20 Targitai married the daughter of the prophet Zoroaster [3b] and brought Cleobulina from Phenicia from his mother, Zoroaster’s wife, the Phoenician queen Busa to Alibant. Here in 753 B.C., at the same time as the founding of Rome (!), Targitay built 3 cities for the Greek Sithons, grain merchants: Olbia-1 near Alibant, Borey (near the Admiralty) and Nison in Broad Beams of Mykolayiv.

The Persian Cleobulina from Targitai had 6 sons and 3 daughters. One of them, Spheno, started reading books and solving charades and riddles at the age of five. Her father, a sailor and poet, brought her books. By the age of 7, she was already reading hidden verses in the Pentateuch of Moses and developed a method of composing hidden Greek acroteleveries, which she called “baskets.” Since then, a tool has emerged in their family to counter their perception of barbarians in Greece, as well as an incentive to create a kindred ancient culture and Christianity, in the writing of which it was possible to hide evidence of their own history and culture. As it turned out, the development of writing, grammar, literature and art was the occupation of the Cimmerian family of Targitay, not the Greeks, from the very beginning.

I will note that in Hades (Mykolayiv) all children in the 3 mentioned cities studied in schools from 8 till 12 years created by Targitay and Spheno on October 4, 728 B.C. This date can be considered the founding day of Ukrainian education.

Targitay fell in love with his 12-year-old daughter Spheno. February 11, 734 B.C. in Olbia-1 they were born the founder of Antiquity and Christianity Arpoxai. The father expelled his son from Hades in 20 and he sailed to Miletus. In 23 Arpoxay received a palm branch for the poem “On Precious Stones”, in 708 B.C. he invented parchment and began its production in Gileya and cut his hair, leaving only part as herring as a sign of exile. In 706 B.C., not the Greeks were elected to the Council of 9 Greece, and in 704 D.C. the king of the Thracian Bosporus, Asporuch, betrothed him to the Roman Claudia.

On October 8, 703 B.C., the day of Atlantis’ death, Arpoxai first reported on it in Athens, and the Greeks did not accept it, saying that “barbarians could not rule us”. August 4, 698 B.C. Arpoxay on parchment published in Athens the first in Europe epic “Arimaspeya” on 400 pages. in 3 books and immediately became famous – Aristeas. October 8, 696 B.C. Targitay and Spheno died near Troy from the Lydian king of Gig. Aristeas and Claudia buried them in the city of Cimmeridi (Seddulbahir), which Targitay founded in 750 B.C.

For 20 years, the oligarch Aristeas, thanks to the trade-in grain, parchment and marble, without the permission of the authorities secretly built on September 28, 675 B.C., the fortress city of Proconnes on the modern island Marble, where he spent the last 35 years of his life. Here Aristeas of Proconneskiy for the last 5 years of his life passed on his remarkable knowledge to Homer, after the exile of his family from Hades by the Scythian Pan in 646 B.C. The coat of arms (“trident”)  of the city Proconnes was the trident of Atlantis. So, the state emblem “trident” of Ukraine around 4000 years! Aristeus of Proconneskiy was a Genius of Geniuses and it is difficult to say that he did not begin in the sciences, art and culture of Antiquity in general.

July 20, 641 B.C. Aristeas made a will in which he handed over three cities in Hades, captured by the Pan, Lyca and Climena, to the parents of Homer, Achilles, Helen and Cleopatra. The oligarch gave all his fortune to Cleopatra and Homer to build a new fortress city, Olbia-2, with a request to keep the name of Olbia-1, where he and his parents were born. For 4 years, together with Homer, he developed a plan of the city walls. Aristeas commanded that Olbia-2 (v. Parutine) be adapted for the arts and trade of the Greeks with the Scythians. In memory of Targitay, he ordered the Olympics to be held every 4 years on his birthday, the first day of the new Cimmerian year and handed over 20 folders with records of Herodotus’ future History to continue writing. The testament became the program of life of Cleopatra and Homer.
